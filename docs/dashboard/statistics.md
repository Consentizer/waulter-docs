# Statistics & Reporting

The Waulter dashboard provides detailed statistics on how visitors interact with your consent banner — from impressions to per-purpose consent rates.

<!-- TODO:
  ## What is tracked
  - **Banner impressions**: how many times the consent banner was displayed
  - **Accept All** interactions: visitors who accepted all purposes
  - **Reject All** interactions: visitors who rejected all purposes
  - **Partial consent** (mixed): visitors who customised their consent choices
  - **Consent withdrawal events**: visitors who revoked previously given consent
  - **Per-purpose consent rates**: acceptance rate for each individual purpose

  ## Whitelisted domains and statistics
  - Only consent events from **whitelisted domains** appear in the dashboard statistics
  - Non-whitelisted domains still fire GCM signals and collect consent — they just don't
    contribute to your statistics reports
  - This means: if you forget to whitelist a domain, consent still works, but you won't
    see stats for that domain
  - See: [Key Concepts — Whitelisted Domains](../getting-started/concepts.md)
-->
