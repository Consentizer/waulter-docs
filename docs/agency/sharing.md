  ## Roles

  ### Read role (view only)
  - Can view configuration settings, statistics, and compliance reports
  - Cannot modify configuration settings or texts
  - Cannot activate/deactivate configurations
  - Best for: client personnel who need to monitor but not change settings

  ### Admin role (full control)
  - Can view and modify all configuration settings
  - Can activate/deactivate configurations
  - Can manage texts, purposes, and styling
  - Best for: client technical teams who manage their own consent setup

  ## How client personnel access shared configs
  - Client logs in to my.waulter.eu/b2b with their credentials
  - Shared configurations appear in their dashboard
  - They can only see/edit configurations shared with them (based on local/global share)
  - Their view is scoped to their role (read or admin)
